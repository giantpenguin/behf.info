Cultivating a Culture of Mindfulness and Enhancing Organizational Performance

Breath Energy Health Foundation

A pioneering social enterprise

BEHF (Breath Energy Health Foundation) is a pioneering social enterprise dedicated to applying meditation and mindfulness as essential skills and capabilities to enhance workplace performance and foster sustainable development in organizations. We provide practical solutions to help businesses build cohesive teams, foster insightful leadership, and create a positive, effective work environment.
Our Operating Principles

Why Choose Us

Benevolence

We are driven by a genuine desire to cultivate kindness and compassion.

Evolution

We believe in the power of mindful practice to foster personal growth, deepen self-awareness, and unlock your inner potential.

Humbleness

We offer guidance without judgment, fostering an environment of acceptance and self-compassion.

Friendship

We believe in fostering a supportive and welcoming space where individuals can connect, learn from each other, and feel a sense of belonging.
